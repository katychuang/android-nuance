Nuance - an android NFC app built at TAPPED, Android NFC Hackathon on October 6-7, 2012. Hackerleague

TAPPED: The NFC Accelerator is a collection of three free events, with one goal: drive innovation of novel NFC mobile app prototypes built on solid business plans.

You're shopping for paint, how do you know which color to get?

With Nuance, you can quickly find out which color swatch is right for you. It also helps you find matching color schemes.
